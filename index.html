Characters

Fin (you) is here to save the world with his big brain. He is able to run, attack and kill all worms on his path. He is someone who wants to show what he knows and what he has achieve. That's why he rewards himself with badges from henchworms and if he defeats a Groutan he will take one tusk of it.

This tinier worms called hecnhworms. I think there is no explanation needed for this designation. Henchworms are not the most dangerous, but some people say there are more henchworms than pigeons. Luckily for you, they only got a brain capacity to remember one topic of your material.

He self is too old to fight in this apocalyps if that's all he wanted. The "Wise-men" give advice to Fin in times Fin's vision is not clear. This storyteller is the reason Fin fights this unexpected apocalyps.

Twelve of these monster are now spread around the world searching for more and more library's to demolish. All started at biggest source of information, the library of Alexandria in Egypt. Already 61% of the information is kept by these Groutans. Kill them.

Storyline

A few hundred years ago there was no internet nor computers. The only storage for all the information were books and our brains. Till today scientist are still researching history and not without reason. All religions always said it: “There will come an apocalypse.” And it came but … not as expected.

Worms came out of the ground and the whole world began to shake. People ran and tried to escape, but no one knew where to go. One strange thing, no one was attacked. Groutans (Giant worms) emerged in Egypt and directly went for the Alexandria Library. It were bookworms. So much information lost that we as humans lost tons of knowledge including knowledge about geography.

Only you can save the world by outsmarting all Groutans and defeating them with your geography knowledge. This apocalyps is a matter of time. Without humans knowledge many living but also unborn creatures will die. You must know, it will be a long journey with some struggle along the way. Very urgent is not to suffer under those struggles and always aim at another solution. Time is ticking my friend.

Do you accept the challenge?

Purpose

Gaming

As you might've read in the storyline there are a few bosses you have to defeat in order to save the world. Before you can fight a Groutan you must take down a few of his henchworms who you shouldn't underestimate. For every henchworm you defeat a badge will be given to you by the population. After killing a Groutan you will take one of his tusks as reward.

Learning

Every chapter in your book got their own trial with each one Groutan. The henchworms consist less subject matter and questions. They do less damage which mean you can make more mistakes. Groutans can be compared to exams and do more damage. Your brain is the mightiest weapon in this battle if you learn your geography.

Fun

Yaawwnn... Sorry I heard someone said wrts practice test. Come on people it is 2022 and some are still using wrts. That makes me want to eat my book. By developing this game we want to achieve an experience far different from all other online tool experiences. We cannot learn for you but we can try to make learning more bearable.

Art-style

Setting


The setting is a fallen world with most of the buildings destroyed due to the earthquakes. All worms digging and moving like maniacs make the air rough and dusty.

Art


Our team chose for a pixel art game. The focus of this game is not on practicing game controls but on learning. Pixel art games are mostly simple to control. Also is the focus of your brain on the moving parts in games. That's why only the important things will move.

Colors


The setting said it all, rough and dusty. Except for Fin and the Wise-men this falling world has a murky vibe and isn't bright-colored at all.
